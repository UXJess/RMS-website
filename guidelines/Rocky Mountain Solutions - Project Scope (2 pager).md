## **Service List for Rocky Mountain Solutions**

**Safety Compliance and Oversight**

* Ensure construction sites follow WorkSafeBC regulations and internal safety policies.  
* Conduct daily, weekly, or scheduled **site safety inspections** to identify hazards and track corrective actions.  
* Create and update **site safety plans, procedures, and safe work permits**.  
* Maintain records of inspections, hazard assessments, and corrective actions.  
* Coordinate with prime contractors, client safety reps, and WorkSafeBC as needed.

**Training and Worker Engagement**

* Lead **safety orientations** for new workers, visitors, and subcontractors.  
* Facilitate **toolbox talks** (brief, regular safety meetings) and other safety briefings.  
* Support site-level health and safety training tailored to specific hazards.  
* Assist with **hazard identification and risk assessment sessions** collaboratively.

**Incident Response and First Aid**

* Act as the **designated first aid attendant** and provide advanced first aid care when needed.  
* Respond to injuries and medical emergencies on site, stabilizing workers before EMS arrives.  
* Manage the first aid station or first aid room, including keeping documentation and updated supply list.  
* Communicate with emergency services and ensure proper reporting to WorkSafeBC and employers.

**Documentation and Reporting**

* Prepare daily, weekly, and monthly **safety reports** for project leadership.  
* Investigate accidents and near misses, collect facts, and recommend corrective actions.  
* Keep detailed records for compliance, audits, and health and safety committees.

**Specialized Safety Functions**

* Perform or coordinate **hazard assessments** and implement mitigation controls.  
* Review and enforce use of personal protective equipment (PPE).  
* Support **COR® (Certificate of Recognition) compliance work** and audits.

**Optional/Value-Added Services**

* Design and deliver **site-specific safety signage and documentation**.  
* Assist with setup and maintenance of safety equipment (e.g., guardrails, safety stations).
