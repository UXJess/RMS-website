# **Rocky Mountain Solutions – Brand Guidelines**

## **1\. Brand Overview**

**Rocky Mountain Solutions** provides professional construction safety and compliance support for job sites across British Columbia.

The company delivers certified Construction Safety Officer services and first aid coverage to ensure job sites operate safely and meet WorkSafeBC requirements.

**Mission:** Rocky Mountain Solutions exists to help construction companies maintain safe, efficient, and compliant work environments.

**Brand Identity:** The brand represents reliability, professionalism, and safety‑first thinking in the construction industry.

## **2\. Brand Principles**

* **Safety First** — Safety is the foundation of every successful job site.  
* **Reliability** — Clients trust Rocky Mountain Solutions to maintain safe and compliant environments.  
* **Professionalism** — Communication and operations are structured, clear, and disciplined.  
* **Directness** — Messaging is clear and factual without unnecessary jargon.

## **3\. Brand Tone**

Communication should feel professional, clear, direct, and confident. Avoid corporate buzzwords, overly promotional language, and unnecessary complexity. Safety communication must always prioritize clarity and accuracy.

## **4\. Logo Introduction**

The Rocky Mountain Solutions logo represents structural reliability and safety. The logo should appear consistently across all communications including:

* Website  
* Marketing materials  
* Safety documentation  
* Construction signage  
* Vehicle branding

## **10\. Typography**

* **Primary Typeface:** Aeonik (Headings, navigation, labels)  
* **Secondary Typeface:** Lora (Editorial body text, pull quotes)  
* **Tertiary Typeface:** Roboto Mono (Data, specifications, labels)

## **11\. Typography Scale**

* **T1 (Display):** 8rem  
* **H1:** 3rem  
* **H2:** 2.25rem  
* **H3:** 1.75rem  
* **Body:** 1.125rem  
* **Button:** 1.15rem  
* **Eyebrow:** 1rem  
* **Small:** 0.875rem

## **12\. Header Spacing**

Headings include `margin‑bottom: 0.25rem`. Applies to: H1, H2, H3, and Eyebrow labels.

## **13\. Line Height**

* **T1:** 1.05 | **H1:** 1.1 | **H2:** 1.2 | **H3:** 1.3  
* **Body:** 1.5 | **Button:** 1.3 | **Eyebrow:** 1.2 | **Small:** 1.4

## **14\. Paragraph Spacing**

Paragraph spacing should be **1rem**. *Example: p { margin-bottom: 1rem; }*

## **15\. Fluid Typography (Clamp)**

* **T1:** clamp(4rem, 6vw, 8rem)  
* **H1:** clamp(2rem, 3vw, 3rem)  
* **H2:** clamp(1.75rem, 2.5vw, 2.25rem)  
* **H3:** clamp(1.5rem, 2vw, 1.75rem)  
* **Body:** clamp(1rem, 1.2vw, 1.125rem)

## **16\. Photography**

Photography should highlight authentic construction environments, workers wearing PPE, and real job sites. **Avoid generic corporate stock imagery.**

## **17\. Layout System**

Layouts should prioritize large typography hierarchy, strong margins, grid‑based layouts, and generous white space. Spacing follows an 8‑point inspired REM spacing system.
