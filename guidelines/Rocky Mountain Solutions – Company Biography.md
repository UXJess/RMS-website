## **Rocky Mountain Solutions – Company Biography** 

After many years working in the construction industry, I shifted my focus toward occupational health and safety after seeing how quickly the demand for qualified safety professionals was growing. Since earning my Construction Safety Officer (CSO) certification, I’ve continued building field experience while expanding my knowledge through ongoing training with accredited providers. 

Throughout this transition, I recognized a clear gap in the industry. Many companies require a qualified CSO or first aid attendant, but not every project justifies a full-time position. At the same time, the Tri-Cities and Fraser Valley continue to grow with new multi-family and commercial developments. As construction moves closer to residential neighborhoods and public spaces, strong safety practices are no longer optional. They are essential for protecting workers, clients, and the public. 

Rocky Mountain Solutions was built on that understanding. The goal is simple: raise the standard of safety culture on every site we step onto. While we continue to provide maintenance, repairs, and renovation services, safety support is a core part of what we do. Our services include daily site inspections, hazard assessments, toolbox talks, incident reporting, risk analysis, and regulatory compliance support. Whether your project requires basic first aid coverage or additional safety oversight on a larger build, we provide practical, reliable support that fits your needs. 

At Rocky Mountain Solutions, safety is not just a requirement to meet. It is a responsibility to lead. Our commitment is to help you create a safer, more accountable job site while keeping your project moving forward with confidence.
